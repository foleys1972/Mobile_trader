# Direct Trader Communications - Business Plan

## Executive Summary

Direct Trader Communications is a professional iOS trading dealerboard platform that replaces legacy turret phone systems with modern mobile technology. The platform enables banks to use their existing Oracle SBC and AudioCodes infrastructure while providing traders with a modern iPhone app supporting three critical line types: Hoot Lines, ARD Lines, and MRD Lines.

## Market Opportunity

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- **Market Size**: $500M+ annually
- **Target Clients**: 1,700+ potential bank clients globally
- **Deployment Advantage**: 2 weeks vs 6 months for legacy systems
- **Cost Savings**: 70% vs traditional turrets

### Competitive Advantages
1. **Modern Technology**: iOS app vs legacy hardware
2. **Rapid Deployment**: 2 weeks vs 6 months
3. **Cost Effective**: 70% cost reduction
4. **White-label Ready**: Supports multiple banks
5. **Zero Infrastructure**: Uses existing bank systems

## Product Overview

### Core Features
- **Hoot Lines**: Always-on, permanent conference calls
- **ARD Lines**: Auto Ring Down with warning tone
- **MRD Lines**: Manual Ring Down (traditional ring/answer)
- **Oracle SBC Integration**: Seamless with existing infrastructure
- **AudioCodes Support**: Full compatibility
- **White-label Ready**: Multi-bank support

### Technical Stack
- **Platform**: iOS (Swift + Linphone SDK)
- **Backend**: FastAPI (Python)
- **Infrastructure**: Railway, Replit
- **VoIP**: Linphone SDK
- **Integration**: Oracle SBC, AudioCodes
